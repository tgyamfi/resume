Event Check-In Kiosk

Greater Houston Partnership is known for holding gatherings of various sizes. From Committee meetings, and discussion panels averaging 10-50 guest. To large scale events and Galas (State of the State, State of City, Annual Meeting, etc.) attracting 500-1200 attendees per event annually.

When guest arrives at the venue they would be directed to a set of tables where they were required to search for their name among a sea of pre-printed badges - hundreds in most cases. Staff members would be within proximity to assist attendees with locating their name badge, but with such a large volume of people, a massive queue quickly formed. Staff were also tasked with attempting tracking the number of guests that arrived based on badges never claimed, all in hopes of getting an approximate attendance count. Post-Event surveys indicated that the issue was one of the single most frustrating parts of an attendee’s overall experience.

Noteworthy

  1. Reduced check-in wait time for event attendees
  2. Reduced staff labor and equipment cost
  3. Improved post-event report data accuracy
  4. Helped the organization track engagement and interest on a per person basis. This data would eventually be combined with other source for targeted marketing efforts and predictive analytics
  5. Allowed the Sales Team to track leads in real time as Non-Members arrived at the venue, via SMS notifications
  6. Improved staff and attendee experience
